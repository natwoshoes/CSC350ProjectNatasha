Borough of Manhattan Community College

I've been attending BMCC since August 2022 and am graduating this May!
The Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is a public institution located in New York. BMCC is best known for its affordability, accessibility, and the amount of majors you can choose from. For the most part I have enjoyed my time here and have met some cool people! I like BMCC because of its small class sizes and location.
